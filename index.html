About Me

Hi! I'm Claire. I live in Northridge, CA with my fiance and our three cats: Kita, Digi, and Kaia. For fun, I enjoy watching sports, running obstacle course races, and working out at my Crossfit box. I graduated from California State University, Los Angeles with a BS in Psychology, and Mount Saint Mary's University, Los Angeles with my MA in Counseling Psychology.

My professional background includes several years working in the non-profit sector serving marginalized communities, such as the LGBTQ, HIV+, and homeless populations. I have experiences in case management, group facilitation, and one-on-one counseling.

Recently, I have decided to pursue to learn a new skill, CODING! I am currently attending UCLA Extention's Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. Wish me luck as I go on this new adventure.
